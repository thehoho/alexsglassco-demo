Quick answers

Do you do autoglass or window frames?
Sorry, no — we specialize in residential & commercial glass, custom mirrors, and frameless shower doors.
How fast can you install?
Often within the same week depending on glass type, measurements, and hardware availability.
Are you licensed?
There’s no specific state license for our trade in Texas. We are fully insured and stand behind our work.
What areas do you serve?
Greater Dallas–Fort Worth: Dallas, Fort Worth, Plano, Frisco, McKinney, Allen, and nearby cities.
Can you replace fogged or cracked window glass?
Yes — we replace insulated, tempered, and low-E panes while keeping your existing frames when possible.
Do you handle custom mirror walls or gym mirrors?
Absolutely — we fabricate and install large mirrors with clean, polished edges and precise seams.
What thickness for frameless showers?
Typically 3/8″ or 1/2″ tempered glass with premium hardware, sized to your opening.
How do quotes work?
Tell us your zip + service, we schedule a quick measure, then deliver a clear, itemized quote — no surprises.
